Uplift Applier
Upload a workbench export, choose an uplift % and promo weeks. The tool calculates how many extra units to register per SKU × Chain × Country on top of the current forecast.
Configure & run
Workbench export (sales + forecast tabs)
📊
Drag or click to upload
Must contain an "Actual sales" tab and a "Forecast" tab
%
Promo period presets
How it works
1
Calculates average weekly sales per SKU × Chain × Country from all weeks in the sales tab
2
Multiplies by (1 + uplift %) to get the target sales level per week
3
Reads the current forecast for the selected promo weeks
4
Output = max(0, target − current forecast) per week — only what's still missing
